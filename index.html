Let's Get Connected!

Follow these simple steps to bring your holiday gift to life.

1

Plug it in

Connect the clock to power. It will start up with a festive glow! Since it doesn't know your home WiFi yet, it will create its own temporary "Hotspot" for you to join.

2

Connect to the Clock

On your phone or laptop, open your WiFi settings and join its special network:

Network Name (SSID)
ESPTimeCast
Password
12345678
3

Configure WiFi

A "Sign In" window might pop up automatically. If not, open a browser and go here:

http://192.168.4.1

Tap Configure WiFi, pick your home network from the list, enter your password, and click Save.

You're Done!

The clock will restart, connect to your home internet, and automatically grab the correct time and local weather. It's a Christmas miracle!

Things You Can Customize

Once connected, use the IP address shown on the clock's display to access its settings menu and make it your own:

  • Weather Location: Set your exact City so the forecast is accurate.
  • Brightness: Adjust the glow or set it to auto-dim at night.
  • Scroll Speed: Control how fast the text and weather move.
  • Preferences: 12h/24h time, F°/C°, and language settings.

Need to make changes later?

Quick Adjustments

Watch the clock face when you first plug it in. It will scroll its new IP address (like 192.168.1.45). Type that into your phone's browser to get back to the settings.

Moved or Changed WiFi?

No problem! If the clock can't connect, it will automatically go back to Step 1 (AP Mode). Just connect to the ESPTimeCast network again to set it up.
